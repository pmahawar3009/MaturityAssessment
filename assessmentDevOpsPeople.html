15ImprovementIs the organization able to react fast when it comes to changes?Changes are only causing failures, therefore we try to limit them as much as possibleChanges are usually implemented too late, mostly due to regulations, procedures and lack of collaboration, leading to long and non-competitive lead timesOur organization is flexible, so we can deal with planned changes in a fast and iterative way, but have some trouble with lead times of unplanned workWe work in an agile way, so we can respond to both planned and unplanned changes in an acceptable time frame, although the delivery chain is not fully automated and can lead to undesirable waiting time before productionOur lead times to both failure recovery and changes are highly competitive and their underlying processes are (almost) fully automated
